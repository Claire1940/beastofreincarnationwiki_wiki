<Section icon={User} title="Beast of Reincarnation Characters and Combat Roles">

The narrative core of this action RPG revolves around two beings who, by the laws of their world, should never coexist. Set in the year 4026, the **Beast of Reincarnation characters** roster is led by Emma, an outcast Sealer, and Koo, a mysterious Malefact dog. Their relationship is not merely a story beat but the foundation of the gameplay loop. Emma handles the high-intensity action, while Koo provides the tactical "brain" of the duo.

Understanding their individual strengths is essential for surviving the blighted landscapes of post-apocalyptic Japan. Emma’s agility and Koo’s destructive techniques must be woven together to overcome the Malefacts that roam the wastes.

| Character | Role | Primary Weapon/Tool | Combat Style |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emma** | Protagonist/Sealer | Katana / Plant-hair | Real-time, fast melee, parries |
| **Koo** | Companion/Malefact | Command Techniques | Tactical, command-based, support |
| **Nushi** | Regional Bosses | Blight Corruption | Area-of-effect, phase-based |
| **The Beast** | Final Threat | Primeval Blight | Unknown / End-game focus |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Synergy">
Successful combat requires using Emma's **Just-Guard parries** to generate Command Points. Once earned, these points allow you to trigger Koo's devastating abilities, effectively bridging the gap between action and strategy.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Users} title="Emma and Koo: The Forbidden Partnership">

The bond between Emma and Koo is the heart of the journey toward the far West. Emma is a Sealer, a person born with the ability to absorb the blight that consumes the world. Because she is fused with plant-like structures, she lacks memories and emotions, leading the survivors in the Colonies to fear her. Koo, conversely, is a Malefact—the very type of creature Sealers are meant to hunt.

<Section icon={Zap} title="Progression: Defeating the Nushi">

The Nushi are the primary antagonists Emma and Koo must face. These are not standard enemies; they are giant Malefacts that act as the source of regional blight. When a Nushi is present, it can transform a peaceful plain into a dense, hostile forest in real time. Defeating these creatures is the only way to acquire the "Nushi Powers" required to reach the final target in the West.

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Boss Phases">

  </Step>
