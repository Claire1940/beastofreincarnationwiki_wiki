<Section icon={Swords} title="Beast of Reincarnation achievements for Combat and Parries">

Combat in Beast of Reincarnation is a unique hybrid system. It combines Emma's fast-paced, real-time katana strikes and defensive parries with Koo's tactical command menu, which slows down the action to let you issue strategic companion skills. To unlock all the combat-focused **Beast of Reincarnation achievements**, you must master the synergy between both characters.

The core of the combat loop relies on reading enemy attack animations and executing perfect parries (Just-Guards) with Emma. A successful parry completely protects Emma from damage and generates critical command points. These points are spent directly from the command menu to unleash Koo's offensive charms and supportive abilities.
