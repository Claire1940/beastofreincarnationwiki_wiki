<Section icon={Map} title="Setting, Lore, and Artistic Style">

The game takes place in Japan in the year 4026. The world has been devastated by a corrupting curse known as the Blight, which has driven humanity to the brink of extinction. Players control Emma, a branded Sealer who possesses unique abilities to interact with the Blight. She is joined by her canine companion, Coup, as they search for a way to halt the destruction.

The narrative explores themes of reincarnation, spiritual cycles, and the delicate relationship between humans, beasts, and technology. The art direction draws heavy inspiration from Studio Ghibli films, particularly Princess Mononoke. This is evident in the design of the corrupt beasts and the spiritual entities that inhabit the world, such as an elk-like creature whose footsteps cause plant life to rapidly grow and decay.

<Section icon={Target} title="Boss Battles and Environmental Hazards">

Boss battles serve as the ultimate test of your mastery over the game's mechanics. You will face a mix of corrupted organic beasts and massive, ancient robots. These encounters are designed to be cinematic and highly challenging, requiring you to adapt to changing environments.

Arenas are not static. During boss fights, the terrain can collapse, weather conditions can shift, and environmental hazards can appear. For example, a boss might destroy a bridge you are standing on, forcing you to use your vines to swing to safety mid-combat. Weather conditions can also affect your movement speed or the effectiveness of certain elemental attacks.
