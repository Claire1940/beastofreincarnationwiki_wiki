The creative force behind the game's music represents a massive departure from traditional projects. This acoustic duality is woven directly into the core combat themes, creating a unique sonic identity for the duo.

<Section icon={Swords} title="How Audio Enhances Combat & Parries">

Combat is a highly rhythmic dance that requires players to balance Emma's real-time katana actions with Koo's tactical command-menu techniques. While visual feedback is important, the game's audio design is a critical tool for mastering the combat loop. Every enemy strike, parry window, and companion ability features a distinct, highly recognizable sound cue designed to guide your inputs.

Hitting a Just-Guard parry relies heavily on reading the auditory tell of an incoming attack. Many malefacts emit a sharp organic growl or a high-pitched metallic glint sound immediately before their strike lands. Once you successfully parry, a resonant metallic ring signals that you have earned command points, allowing you to open the command menu. Opening this menu slows the action down and filters the audio, muting high frequencies so you can focus on selecting the best companion skill.
