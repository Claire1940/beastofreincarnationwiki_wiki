Setting up cross-progression requires minimal configuration due to the integration with the Xbox network. When playing on Xbox Series X|S, game progress, character statistics, unlocked skills, and collected spirit stones are automatically uploaded to the cloud upon exiting a session. When switching to a Windows PC, the Xbox application retrieves the latest save file from the cloud, allowing players to resume their journey exactly where they left off.

<Callout type="warning" title="Sync Warning">

</Callout>
