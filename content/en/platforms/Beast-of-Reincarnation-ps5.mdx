<Section icon={Target} title="Hunting the Nushi: Evolution Mechanics">

The core loop of **Beast of Reincarnation ps5** revolves around the hunt. Nushi are not static bosses; they are living entities that react to the player's playstyle. If you rely too heavily on fire damage, the Nushi may evolve a heat-resistant hide mid-battle. This dynamic difficulty system ensures that no two hunts feel exactly the same.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Tracking and Identification">
    Use the Purifier's Sense to find spiritual trails left by the Nushi. Identify the beast's current stage to prepare the correct elemental counters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Initial Engagement">
    Engage the beast to lower its health to the first threshold. Focus on removing external armor pieces to expose the core.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Evolution Management">
    As the Nushi begins to evolve, use Purification Stones to stall the transformation. This is the most dangerous phase of the fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Purification">
    Once the beast is weakened, execute the Purification Ritual. Success grants rare materials used for crafting high-tier reincarnation gear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Understanding the evolution tiers is vital for progression. Lower-tier Nushi may only evolve once, while "Alpha" variants can undergo three distinct transformations, each changing their move set entirely.

| Evolution Tier | Mutation Frequency | Danger Level | Reward Quality |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lesser Nushi** | 0-1 Times | Low | Common Shards |
| **Greater Nushi** | 1-2 Times | Medium | Rare Essence |
| **Ancient Nushi** | 2-3 Times | High | Epic Cores |
| **Evolved Deity** | Constant | Extreme | Legendary Souls |
