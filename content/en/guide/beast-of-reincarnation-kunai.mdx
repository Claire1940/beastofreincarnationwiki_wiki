- **Parry Window**: You can deflect incoming projectiles by timing a Kunai throw just before impact.
- **Distraction**: Throwing a Kunai near an unalerted enemy will cause them to investigate the sound, allowing for a stealthy bypass.
- **Escape**: Using the "Grapple Hook" attribute allows you to pull yourself toward a Kunai embedded in a wall, escaping dangerous ground effects.
