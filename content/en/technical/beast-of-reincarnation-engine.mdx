<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics & Vertical Traversal">

The core combat loop of **Beast of Reincarnation** heavily incorporates elements from classic Souls-like games, requiring precise timing, stamina management, and careful positioning. However, it distinguishes itself through incredible environmental verticality and organic traversal systems.

### Combat Archetypes Comparison

| Attribute | Ground Combat | Aerial Combat | Stealth Approach |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Primary Focus** | Deflection & Countering | High-Impact Drop Attacks | Silent Takedowns |
| **Risk Level** | Medium (Requires parry timing) | High (Vulnerable to anti-air) | Low (Safest engagement) |
| **Mobility Speed** | Standard | Extremely Fast | Slow & Calculated |
| **Ideal Targets** | Elite Knights & Bosses | Grouped Minions | Isolated Patrols |

### Environmental Manipulation

Players can interact directly with the forest flora to alter the battlefield. Special seeds can be thrown or activated in proximity to sprout massive, glowing flowers. These flowers act as organic launching pads, allowing the player to slingshot into the air to escape danger or initiate aerial finishers.

During specific segments of the game, players will coordinate with a giant, piloted mech. The protagonist can ride inside or fight alongside this unit, combining nimble swordplay with heavy-duty firepower.
