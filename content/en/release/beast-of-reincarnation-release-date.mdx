<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and Parry Guide">

The combat loop of *Beast of Reincarnation* is built around a dynamic partnership. You directly control Emma, utilizing fast-paced katana strikes, dodges, and high-stakes parries in real time. Meanwhile, Koo acts as your tactical support companion. By opening the command menu during battle, the action slows down to a crawl, allowing you to strategically select Koo's specialized abilities.

To utilize Koo's skills, you must generate command points by playing defensively with Emma. Successfully executing a "Just-Guard" parry is the primary method for earning these points. This system seamlessly blends high-intensity action with tactical decision-making.

| Combat Action | Primary Input (PS5) | Resource Effect | Tactical Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Katana Strike** | Square / R2 | Generates basic stamina | Deals consistent close-range damage |
| **Just-Guard Parry** | L1 (Precise Timing) | Generates Koo Command Points | Negates damage and creates counter openings |
| **Command Menu** | Triangle | Slows real-time action | Allows tactical selection of Koo's skills |
| **Koo Command** | D-Pad / Face Buttons | Consumes Command Points | Unleashes companion support or offensive skills |

<Callout type="tip" title="Master the Slowdown">
Do not hesitate to open the Command Menu frequently. The dramatic slowdown effect does not penalize your combat rating and gives you crucial moments to assess enemy positioning and plan your next move.
</Callout>

To get the most out of this hybrid combat system, players should practice the core mechanical flow as early as possible. Follow these steps to master the basic flow:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Observe Enemy Wind-ups">
    Watch your opponent closely. Avoid spamming standard attacks when a Malefact begins its attack animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Time the Just-Guard">
    Press the block button right before the enemy strike connects. A successful parry will flash gold and instantly fill your command gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger the Command Menu">
    Open the tactical interface to slow down time. Analyze the battlefield, checking for enemy vulnerabilities or environmental hazards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Koo's Technique">
    Spend your generated points to unleash one of Koo's active abilities, then immediately follow up with Emma's katana to maximize damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Target} title="World Exploration and Progression">

Set in the year 4026, the game presents a ruined vision of Japan where nature, ancient myths, and decayed technology collide. The world is heavily infected by a corrupting force known as the Blight. As a Sealer, Emma must travel westward alongside Koo to absorb this corruption and defeat the giant Malefacts, called Nushi, that control the transformed regions.

Exploration is highly dynamic. Plains and wastelands can transform in real time under the influence of the Blight, suddenly sprouting dense, dangerous forests filled with hostile creatures. To navigate this changing terrain, Emma utilizes her plant-fused physical traits, allowing her to traverse vertical structures and discover hidden pathways.
