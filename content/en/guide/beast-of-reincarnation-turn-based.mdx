<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Mastering Koo Turn Based Commands">

When the FP gauge in the lower right corner of the screen is completely filled, you gain access to Bloom Arts. Activating a Bloom Art triggers a slow-motion phase, mimicking traditional turn-based RPG command menus. This tactical pause allows you to evaluate the battlefield, predict enemy movements, and select the most effective technique without the pressure of real-time threats.

You can customize your overall approach by selecting loadouts that emphasize different aspects of combat. Depending on your preference, you can lean into aggressive, ranged, or stealthy setups.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Boss Battle Deflection Strategies">

Each region in the game is guarded by a massive boss enemy. These encounters test your ability to combine real-time reflexes with tactical command selections. Because boss attacks deal massive damage, relying solely on dodging is rarely sufficient. You must learn to deflect their heaviest blows to build FP quickly.

During boss fights, the slow-motion window provided by Bloom Arts is invaluable. Use this time to observe the boss's preparatory movements and select a counter-measure, such as canceling a major attack with binding skills.

| Boss State | Behavior | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Neutral** | Standard attack patterns and physical strikes | Parry to build FP and chip at the down gauge |
| **Preparing Big Move** | Long wind-up animation with high damage potential | Use Binding Roots to cancel the attack |
| **Stunned / Downed** | Vulnerable, takes increased damage | Unleash all stored FP and trigger a finisher |

Before entering a boss arena, ensure you have optimized your setup. A prepared player has a much higher chance of surviving these brutal encounters.
