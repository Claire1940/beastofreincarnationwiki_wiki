<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Combat and Parry System">

The combat in **Beast of Reincarnation Emma** is a unique blend of high-speed action and tactical decision-making. While you control Emma in real-time, accessing Koo's skills requires a deliberate pause in the action. Successful combat relies on the "Just-Guard" parry system, which acts as the bridge between Emma's physical attacks and Koo's supernatural abilities.

| Combat Action | Mechanic Type | Result / Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light/Heavy Attack** | Real-Time | Sustained damage and enemy pressure |
| **Just-Guard Parry** | Timing-Based | Blocks damage and generates **Command Points** |
| **Koo's Command Menu** | Tactical Pause | Slows time to select companion techniques |
| **Plant-Hair Whip** | Real-Time | Mid-range crowd control and gap closing |
| **Falling Assassination** | Stealth | High-damage opening strike from elevated terrain |

<Callout type="warning" title="Parry Timing">
Parrying is not just defensive; it is your primary resource generator. Without successful parries, you will find yourself unable to use Koo's most powerful techniques during boss fights.
</Callout>

The flow of battle generally follows a specific rhythm: observe the enemy, parry their strike, open the command menu, and execute a Koo technique to exploit the opening.

</Section>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Best Builds and Equipment for Emma">

Customizing **Beast of Reincarnation Emma** involves balancing three core components: Katanas, Spirit Stones, and Koo's Charms. Each build alters how you interact with the blight and which combat style becomes most effective.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Base Weapon">
    Choose a Katana that fits your playstyle. The **Big Dipper** (available in the Digital Deluxe Edition) offers balanced stats, while exploration-found blades may focus on speed or parry windows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Socket Spirit Stones">
    Spirit Stones provide passive bonuses triggered by specific actions. For an aggressive build, focus on stones that grant attack buffs after a successful Just-Guard.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Koo's Charms">
    Charms modify Koo's command skills. Some increase the area of effect for his attacks, while others reduce the Command Point cost of defensive skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Skill Tree Nodes">
    Prioritize the "Resonance" branch of the skill tree to improve the synergy between Emma's parries and Koo's follow-up attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Build Style | Primary Focus | Key Equipment / Skills |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aggressive Melee** | High DPS | Fast Katanas, Attack-buff Spirit Stones |
| **Tactical Defense** | Parry Windows | Heavy Charms, Parry-window extension nodes |
| **Ranged Support** | Koo Commands | Command Point regeneration stones, AoE Charms |
| **Stealth Stalker** | Assassinations | Verticality skills, Movement speed Spirit Stones |

<Callout type="tip" title="Build Flexibility">
You can reset your skill tree at specific Colony locations. Don't be afraid to experiment with different Spirit Stone combinations when facing a new Nushi.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Exploration and World Traversal">

The Japan of 4026 is a vertical and ever-changing landscape. Emma's plant-like hair is her greatest tool for navigating the ruins of the old world. From crossing collapsed bridges to scaling the walls of destroyed skyscrapers, exploration is just as important as combat.

| Traversal Feature | Description | Gameplay Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vine Grapple** | Emma extends her hair to latch onto points | Crossing large gaps and ascending ruins |
| **Wall Run** | Plant-fused limbs provide extra grip | Navigating narrow urban alleys |
| **Blight Transformation** | Environment changes in real-time | Opens new routes as forests emerge |
| **Hidden Routes** | Vertical paths above the main road | Finding rare Katanas and Spirit Stones |

<Callout type="tip" title="Look Up">
Ancient legends in the game warn players never to look at the sky. However, using Emma's verticality to stay above the ground is often the safest way to avoid unnecessary malefact encounters.
</Callout>
