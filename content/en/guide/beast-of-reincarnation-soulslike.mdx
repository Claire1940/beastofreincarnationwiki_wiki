<Section icon={Target} title="Analyzing the Souls-like Criteria">

To determine if **beast of reincarnation soulslike** is an accurate label, we must look at the specific criteria defined by the hardcore community. A "true" Souls-like is often characterized by more than just difficulty; it involves a specific philosophy of player failure and world-building.

One of the most debated aspects is the "irremovable threat." In traditional RPGs, you can over-level to the point where early-game enemies deal zero damage. In a Souls-like, even a level 1 mob can kill a level 300 player if they become careless. *Beast of Reincarnation* appears to lean into this philosophy by emphasizing "technical" skill over raw stat progression.
