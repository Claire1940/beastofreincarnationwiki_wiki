<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and the Parry System">

The core of **Beast of Reincarnation game freak** lies in the unique partnership between Emma, a plant-hybrid Sealer, and Koo, a mysterious canine malefact. Combat is a dual-layered system that blends high-speed melee action with tactical decision-making. Emma handles the real-time swordplay, utilizing her vine-like hair for mobility and her katana for rapid strikes.

The "Just-Guard" parry is the bridge between these two styles. By timing a block perfectly against an enemy strike, Emma generates Command Points. These points are spent to activate Koo’s powerful techniques. When you open the command menu, the action slows down significantly, giving you the breathing room to select the best support skill for the current situation.

| Combat Layer | Control Focus | Primary Resource |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emma (Action)** | Real-time Katana strikes, Dodging, Parrying | Stamina / Health |
| **Koo (Command)** | Tactical support, Area of Effect attacks, Healing | Command Points |
| **Synergy** | Combined finishers and environmental assassinations | Resonance Meter |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastering the Flow">
Do not treat Koo's commands as separate attacks. Instead, use them to extend Emma's combos or to interrupt enemy "Red Attacks" that cannot be parried. The slowed-down menu is your best tool for assessing the battlefield when surrounded by multiple Malefacts.
</Callout>

Players can customize their approach using Spirit Stones. these items provide passive bonuses triggered by specific actions, such as gaining health after a successful parry or increasing movement speed after a dodge. Balancing your Spirit Stone loadout with Emma’s katana upgrades is essential for surviving the higher difficulty settings.

</Section>

  <Step num="2" title="Master the Verticality">

  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Koo's First Skill Branch">
