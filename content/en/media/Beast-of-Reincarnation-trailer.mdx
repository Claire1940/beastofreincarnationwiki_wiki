| Combat Action | Input / Trigger | Tactical Result |

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Combat Loop Tutorial">

To succeed in the blighted forests shown in the promotional footage, players must master the connection between Emma's sword attacks and Koo's supportive magic.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Read Enemy Animations">
    Observe the wind-up and direction of incoming strikes. Avoid attacking blindly when an opponent begins a clear offensive animation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Execute a Just-Guard Parry">
    Press the defensive input right as the strike reaches Emma. A successful parry mitigates damage and generates points for Koo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Open the Tactical Command Menu">
    Activate the command menu to slow down the action. This pause allows you to evaluate the battlefield and select Koo's next move safely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy Koo's Support Skill">
    Spend your accumulated points to execute one of Koo's equipped Charms, targeting specific enemy weaknesses or shielding Emma.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Capitalize on the Opening">
    Return to real-time action immediately. Unleash Emma's high-speed katana combos while the enemy is recovering from Koo's intervention.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Build Customization">
You can further customize this loop by equipping Spirit Stones found throughout the world, which grant passive buffs triggered by specific combat actions.
</Callout>
