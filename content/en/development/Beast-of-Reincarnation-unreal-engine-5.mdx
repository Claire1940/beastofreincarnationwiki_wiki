<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Optimize Performance on Launch Day">

If you experience frame drops during heavy combat sequences or when entering a newly spawned Blighted Forest, follow these steps to optimize your settings.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enable Upscaling">
    Navigate to the Graphics menu and turn on Temporal Super Resolution (TSR), DLSS, or FSR. Setting this to "Quality" offers a massive performance boost with minimal impact on image clarity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Lower Foliage Quality">
    Because the game renders dense forests dynamically, lowering Foliage Quality from Ultra to High or Medium significantly reduces CPU and GPU overhead in blighted areas.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Adjust Shadow Quality">
    Unreal Engine 5's shadow systems are highly detailed but demanding. Reducing shadow resolution or turning off local ray-traced shadows will stabilize frame rates during boss fights.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Limit Background Applications">
    The engine is highly threaded and relies heavily on CPU performance to stream assets. Close background browsers and recording software to free up system resources.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
