### Core Combat Foundations

Emma possesses standard light and heavy attacks, a guard, and a dodge. However, parrying serves as the primary engine for your combat efficiency. Succeeding with parries is the only way to build up both Emma's and Ku's specialized ability gauges.

| Combat Mechanic | Primary Function | Gauge Impact |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Perfect Parry** | Deflects incoming attacks and builds combat gauges | Increases both Emma's and Ku's gauges |
| **Standard Guard** | Reduces incoming damage but drains defense | Decreases Emma's gauge with each block |
| **Perfect Dodge** | Avoids red-highlighted unblockable attacks | No direct gauge impact, repositioning tool |
| **Stagger Meter** | Depleted by attacks and parries to trigger executions | Triggers high-damage chain executions |

<Callout type="warning" title="Gauge Depletion Warning">
If Emma's defensive gauge is completely depleted through constant guarding or taking direct hits, she will enter a temporary staggered state. During this window, you are completely vulnerable and will take massive damage from enemy follow-up attacks.
</Callout>

### Status Effects and Elemental Build-Ups

Enemies possess dedicated status meters that fill up when hit with specific elements. You must coordinate Emma's ranged ammo and Ku's skills to trigger these powerful debuffs.

| Element / Status | Primary Combat Effect | Best Applied By |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Burn** | Inflicts damage over time | Ku's fire skills / Fire ammo |
| **Shock** | Stuns and interrupts enemy actions | Ku's lightning abilities |
| **Poison** | Slowly drains health pools | Poison arrows / Crossbow bolts |
| **Corrosion** | Lowers enemy defense ratings | Specialized elemental skills |

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Tip">
Use Emma's plant creation skills to cluster enemies together, then trigger Ku's crowd control abilities to root them in place before launching a high-damage area-of-effect elemental attack.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Character Progression and Customization">

Both Emma and Ku feature completely independent skill trees and inventories. You must allocate Skill Points (SP) earned from combat to unlock active abilities and passive stat boosts for each character individually.

### Build Customization Slots

Emma's inventory allows you to customize your playstyle through weapons, armor, and soul stones.

| Gear Category | Customization Impact | Upgrade Path |
|--- |--- |--- |
| **Swords** | Grants unique passives that trigger upon successful parries | Enhances base damage and passive effects |
| **Ranged Weapons** | Bows (long-range precision) vs Hand Crossbows (rapid-fire lock-on) | Unlocks and improves diverse ammo types |
| **Gambeson (Armor)** | Single armor piece providing defense and unique passives | Increases defense and unlocks soul stone slots |
| **Soul Stones** | Modifies crit chance, parry windows, lifesteal, and build-up rates | Upgradable to scale passive bonuses |

### Step-by-Step Build Optimization

Follow these steps to establish a balanced build early in your playthrough.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire SP and Unlock Skills">

  <Step num="2" title="Equip Synergy Gear">

  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Nushi Bosses">
    Hunt down the regional Nushi bosses to absorb powerful, progression-locked skills that add unique plant-based utility and high elemental damage to your loadout.

<Section icon={Map} title="Exploration and Base Operations">

The world of Beast of Reincarnation is structured as multiple vast, vertical quarantine zones rather than a seamless open world. You navigate between these zones using a massive, customizable mobile mech.

### Base Operations inside the Mech

Your mobile mech serves as your central hub. Here, you can prepare for upcoming expeditions by utilizing various progression systems.

- **Cooking**: Combine gathered ingredients to prepare meals that grant temporary stat boosts and elemental resistances.
- **Crafting**: Use materials dropped by enemies to forge new ammunition, items, and gear upgrades.
- **Researching**: Unlock new recipes, blueprints, and upgrade pathways for both your characters and the mech itself.
- **Mech Upgrades**: Enhance the mech's capabilities to unlock new traversal and utility options across quarantine zones.

### Traversal and Metroidvania Elements

Exploration relies heavily on verticality and unlocking new movement abilities. Areas feature multiple floors filled with environmental puzzles, locked chests requiring codes, and hidden paths.
