<Section icon={Map} title="Character Customization and Visual Progression">

In the devastated Japan of 4026, visual identity reflects the bond between the Sealer and her companion. **Beast of Reincarnation outfits** serve as both a mark of progression and a way for players to personalize their journey toward the far West. Unlike traditional MMOs, customization here is tightly integrated into the "one-person, one-dog" action RPG framework, focusing on Emma's gear and Koo's unique skins.

The game's aesthetic blends traditional Japanese elements with post-apocalyptic ruins. Emma, a protagonist fused with plant-like blight, features a design that evolves as she absorbs power from defeated Nushi. While her base silhouette is defined by her vine-like hair and nomadic attire, players can modify her appearance through specific equipment slots and deluxe cosmetic items.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Emma's Equipment and Weapon Visuals">

Emma's primary visual customization comes through her katanas and the Spirit Stones she equips. Each katana model is distinct, reflecting the region or Nushi from which it was obtained. As a Sealer, Emma's combat style is fast and precise, and her weapon is the centerpiece of her design.

Beyond the katanas, **Spirit Stones** play a dual role. While they primarily provide passive combat bonuses, they also trigger visual effects during specific actions. For example, stones that trigger on a "Just-Guard Parry" may cause Emma's plant-hair to pulse with golden light or emit embers.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Koo's Skins and Companion Customization">

Koo is more than just a pet; he is a Malefact companion with his own progression and customization system. **Beast of Reincarnation outfits** for Koo are primarily handled through "Skins" which change his entire breed or fur pattern, and "Charms" which act as smaller accessories.

The Shiba skins are the current focus of the launch rewards. The **Brown Shiba** offers a warm, traditional look, while the **Black Shiba** provides a more aggressive, stealthy aesthetic. These skins do not change Koo's combat stats but allow players to distinguish their companion while navigating the blighted forests of Japan.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Menu">
    Open the character management screen and navigate to the 'Companion' tab.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Skins">
    Choose from the available skins like the Brown or Black Shiba to change Koo's base model.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Charms">
    Select up to two Charms found during exploration to add small visual details to Koo's collar or harness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Changes">
    Apply the changes to see the new look reflected instantly in the game world.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Note">
Koo's command-based techniques also have unique animations. While skins don't change the skills, the visual contrast of a Black Shiba performing a glowing holy attack can be quite striking.
</Callout>

</Section>
