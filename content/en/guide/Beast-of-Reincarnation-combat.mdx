<Section icon={Swords} title="The Hybrid Combat Loop: Action Meets Tactics">

The core of **Beast of Reincarnation combat** is a unique "one-person, one-dog" system that bridges the gap between high-speed action and tactical decision-making. Set in a post-apocalyptic Japan in 4026, the game requires you to manage two distinct styles simultaneously. Emma, the Sealer, provides the physical presence on the battlefield with her katana, while her companion Koo, a powerful malefact, acts as a tactical extension of her will.

Understanding the relationship between these two characters is the first step toward mastery. Emma’s movements are fluid and real-time, focusing on positioning, basic combos, and defensive maneuvers. Koo, however, is controlled primarily through a command menu that triggers a "slow-motion" state. This transition allows you to pause the chaos, observe enemy telegraphs, and spend the resources Emma has earned through her swordplay.

| **Passive Effects** | Both | Automatic | Triggered by Spirit Stones and Charms |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Mastering the Just-Guard and Parry Mechanics">

If there is one mechanic that separates beginners from experts in **Beast of Reincarnation combat**, it is the Just-Guard. Because Koo’s most powerful techniques require command points, you cannot simply wait for them to recharge over time. You must actively "earn" them by engaging with the enemy's offense.

A Just-Guard is performed by pressing the defensive input just as an enemy strike is about to make contact with Emma. Unlike a standard block, which may still result in chip damage or stamina loss, a successful parry completely nullifies the attack and grants a significant boost to your command meter. This creates a rhythmic flow: Emma defends to empower Koo, and Koo attacks to create openings for Emma.

**Combat Flow Sequences:**
1. **Observe**: Watch for the enemy's wind-up animation or visual "glint."
2. **React**: Execute the Just-Guard at the moment of impact.
3. **Convert**: Use the generated points to open the command menu.
4. **Execute**: Select a Koo technique that exploits the enemy's current state (e.g., a stun or a knockback).
5. **Follow-up**: Switch back to Emma to land high-damage katana combos while the enemy is reeling.

| Parry Timing | Result | Command Point Gain |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Too Early** | Standard Block | Minimal / None |
| **Just-Guard** | Perfect Parry | High |
| **Too Late** | Take Damage | Zero |
| **Counter-Hit** | Interruption | Moderate |

<Callout type="warning" title="Stamina Management">
Continuous blocking without timing will drain Emma's stamina quickly. In blighted forests where enemies are aggressive, relying on standard blocks will leave you vulnerable to guard-breaks. Always aim for the Just-Guard.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Zap} title="Koo's Command Techniques and Tactical Support">

While Emma provides the blade, Koo provides the "magic." As a malefact, Koo possesses abilities that can alter the environment and debilitate even the largest Nushi (bosses). These techniques are categorized into offensive, defensive, and utility charms.

The command menu is designed to be intuitive. On PlayStation 5, for example, holding the Triangle button brings up a radial or list-based menu. While this menu is active, the game world slows to a crawl, but it does not stop entirely. You must still act with relative speed, especially against faster bosses who can still close the distance during the slowdown.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Momentum">
    Engage in basic katana combat with Emma to find your rhythm and look for parry opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Generate Points">
    Successfully execute 2-3 Just-Guards to fill at least one segment of the command bar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Technique">
    Open the menu and choose a skill like "Howling Blast" for area damage or "Canine Bind" to root a fast-moving target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Synergize">
    Immediately follow Koo's attack with Emma’s "Falling Assassination" or a heavy plant-hair strike for maximum damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combo Potential">
Certain Koo skills apply "Blight Vulnerability." If Emma attacks an enemy in this state, her katana strikes deal 1.5x damage and have a higher chance of staggering the foe.
</Callout>

<Section icon={BookOpen} title="Environmental Combat and Nushi Boss Battles">

Combat in *Beast of Reincarnation* is not limited to flat arenas. The "Blight" transforms the world in real-time, turning open plains into dense, vertical forests. Emma’s plant-like hair is a combat tool as much as a traversal one. You can use it to swing across collapsed bridges or pull yourself toward a flying enemy.

Nushi, the giant regional bosses, represent the ultimate test of your combat knowledge. These encounters often feature multiple phases where the environment changes mid-fight. You must use everything you've learned—parrying their massive strikes, using Koo to disable their shields, and navigating the terrain to avoid area-of-effect attacks.
