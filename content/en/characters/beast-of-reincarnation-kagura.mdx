### Mastering the Dodge Offset

An advanced technique for the Kagura style is the dodge offset. This allows Emma to initiate a purification dance, dodge an incoming attack mid-animation, and resume the dance from the exact step she left off. Mastering this technique is crucial for maintaining high damage per second (DPS) during chaotic encounters where enemies attack relentlessly.
