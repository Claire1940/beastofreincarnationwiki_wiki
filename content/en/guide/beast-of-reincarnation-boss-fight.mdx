<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Boss Mechanics and Phase Breakdown">

The **beast of reincarnation boss fight** is divided into three distinct phases, each requiring a shift in tactical focus. Failure to adapt to these shifts usually results in a quick party wipe.

### Phase 1: The Primal Form (100% - 70% HP)
In this initial stage, the Beast relies on physical prowess. It uses sweeping claw attacks and a forward-facing roar that silences players for 3 seconds. The goal here is to maintain a steady DPS rotation while preserving stamina for the upcoming transitions.

### Phase 2: The Ethereal Shift (70% - 30% HP)
Once the boss hits 70% HP, it becomes semi-transparent and gains a 50% resistance to physical damage. It will begin summoning Soul Orbs at the edges of the arena. If these orbs reach the boss, it recovers 5% of its maximum health per orb.

| Attack Name | Visual Cue | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Cleave** | Blue glow on the Beast's right arm | Dash to the left immediately |
| **Rebirth Shockwave** | Boss leaps to the center and hovers | Jump or use an invincibility frame |
| **Orb Summon** | Three purple portals appear | Split DPS to destroy orbs instantly |
| **Spectral Breath** | Boss inhales deeply | Move behind the boss's legs |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
In Phase 2, do not use all your mobility skills at once. The Beast often follows a Soul Cleave with a Shockwave, requiring two consecutive dodges.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Tactical Execution">

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth victory in the **beast of reincarnation boss fight**. This sequence is designed for a standard four-player party but can be adapted for solo play with higher-tier gear.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Opening Engagement">
    The tank should engage the boss first, pulling it to the north edge of the arena. This keeps the center clear for players to react to the first Rebirth Shockwave. DPS should focus on building their resource bars without using ultimates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Managing the 70% Transition">
    As the boss nears 70% HP, stop all heavy attacks. Wait for the Beast to enter its invulnerability animation. Once it turns ethereal, the DPS must immediately move to the perimeter to intercept the first wave of Soul Orbs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Handling the 30% Enrage">
    At 30% HP, the boss enters its Final Form, combining physical and ethereal attacks. It will also summon a permanent fog that deals minor damage over time. Use your healing ultimates here and unleash all saved offensive abilities to end the fight quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Mechanic Note">
The fog in Phase 3 cannot be cleansed. It is a soft-enrage mechanic designed to prevent the fight from lasting too long. You must out-damage the ticking health loss.
</Callout>

</Section>
