<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering Blades, Bows, and Botanical Arts">

Combat is fast-paced and highly tactical, requiring players to balance close-range attacks, long-range support, and companion abilities. Emma is armed with a katana for rapid-fire melee strikes, but she can also switch to bows and crossbows to deal elemental damage from a safe distance.

The real depth of combat lies in Emma's synergy with Ku. Ku can unleash Bloom Arts, which are nature-based spells that alter the flow of battle. For example, using Higonbana roots can bind groups of enemies together, setting them up for massive area-of-effect damage. Performing well-timed Quick Time Events (QTEs) during these arts will also restore Emma's health, rewarding aggressive and precise playstyles.

<Callout type="tip" title="Parry to Survive">
Defensive play is highly rewarded. Parrying attacks builds the Fluorescent Points needed to unleash Ku's devastating Bloom Arts.
</Callout>

| Combat Element | Mechanic Style | Primary Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Katana | Melee / Slashing | High-speed close-quarters combat and basic combos |
| Bows & Crossbows | Ranged / Projectile | Safe distance damage with customizable elemental ammo |
| Bloom Arts | Companion Skills | Status effects, crowd control, and healing via QTEs |
| Parrying | Defensive Timing | Negates damage and generates essential Fluorescent Points |
