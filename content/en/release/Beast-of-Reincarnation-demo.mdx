<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Core Combat & Parry Loop">

Combat in the **Beast of Reincarnation demo** relies on a seamless loop between Emma's real-time swordplay and Koo's command-based assistance. You cannot win difficult encounters by button-mashing; instead, you must learn to read enemy attack animations and time your defensive actions perfectly.

| Combat Phase | Primary Action | Core Mechanic | Player Focus |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Phase 1: Defense | Read the incoming attack | Just-Guard parrying | Watch enemy wind-up animations |
| Phase 2: Command | Slow down real-time combat | Open Koo's command menu | Assess the battlefield layout |
| Phase 3: Execute | Spend generated points | Activate Koo's techniques | Match skills to enemy weaknesses |
| Phase 4: Follow-up | Return to real-time action | Emma's katana strikes | Exploit openings created by Koo |

<Callout type="warning" title="Parrying is Mandatory">
Failing to parry means you will not generate enough command points to activate Koo's support skills. Treat parrying as your primary offensive builder rather than just a defensive option.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Your First Demo Run">

To make the most of your time in the **Beast of Reincarnation demo**, follow this step-by-step progression path. This sequence ensures you learn the fundamental systems of exploration, plant-hair traversal, and combat before taking on the major bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Difficulty">
    Choose a comfortable difficulty setting. If you are new to hybrid action-tactical RPGs, starting on a lower difficulty lets you practice parry timings without getting punished too severely by basic malefacts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Practice Plant-Hair Traversal">
    Explore the environment using Emma's vine-like hair. Practice crossing collapsed bridges, climbing high walls, and reaching elevated platforms to find hidden routes and equipment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute a Falling Assassination">
    Locate an enemy patrolling below an elevated position. Use Emma's plant hair to extend over the target and execute a falling assassination to eliminate or heavily damage them before direct combat starts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Engage in Parry Training">
    Find an isolated malefact and focus entirely on defensive timing. Press your guard button right before the enemy's strike connects to achieve a Just-Guard parry and fill your command meter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Deploy Koo's Techniques">
    Once your meter is full, open Koo's command menu to slow down the action. Select an offensive or crowd-control technique, then immediately follow up with Emma's rapid katana strikes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Exploration Rewards">
Do not rush directly to the end of the demo area. Exploring ruined urban structures and blighted paths can reward you with specialized katanas for Emma and protective charms for Koo.
</Callout>

</Section>
