<Section icon={Map} title="How to Start Your Reincarnation Journey">

Navigating the post-apocalyptic world of 4026 requires more than just knowing the **Beast of Reincarnation price**. Players must prepare for a steep learning curve that shifts from traditional RPG tropes to punishing Soulslike encounters. The following steps outline the optimal way to begin your journey and avoid common early-game pitfalls.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup and Class Choice">
    After purchasing the game, focus on the initial character calibration. While there are no hard classes, your starting "Blight Resonance" determines your initial plant powers. Choose carefully, as respec costs are high in the early game.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mastering the Vine QTE">
    The game utilizes Quick Time Events (QTE) for its vine-swinging traversal. Spend time in the opening "Ghibli Forest" area to master the rhythm, as missing a swing in later vertical zones leads to instant death.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Bonding with the Mutant Pup">
    Your companion is not just a cosmetic pet. Feed it "Corrupted Spores" found in the world to level up its AI. A higher-level pup is significantly less likely to suffer from the pathing glitches reported by early testers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeating Emma's First Blight">
    The first major boss serves as a skill check for the parry system. Do not rely on menu-mashing; focus on learning the telegraphs of the boss's vine attacks to succeed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Save Management">
Due to reported bugs where items can "duplicate or vanish," it is highly recommended to manually backup your save files after every major boss encounter or significant loot drop.
</Callout>
