<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay: The Purifier vs. The Nushi">

The narrative core of Beast of Reincarnation revolves around the Purifier's eternal struggle against the Nushi. As revealed in the announce trailer, the mission is simple: find and defeat the Nushi. However, the gameplay is complicated by the fact that these creatures do not stay static. They evolve based on the environment and the damage they take, making every encounter unique.

The combat system utilizes a mix of fast-paced hack-and-slash mechanics and tactical positioning. Players must manage their "Reincarnation Gauge," which allows them to revive or unleash powerful finishing moves at the cost of permanent world-state changes.

| Mechanic | Description | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Nushi Evolution | Enemies transform mid-fight | Requires dynamic tactic changes |
| Soul Siphoning | Absorbing fallen enemy cores | Unlocks new skill tree branches |
| Purifier Arts | Special class-based abilities | High damage, long cooldowns |
| Environmental Synergy | Using terrain to trap beasts | Essential for high-tier boss fights |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Insight">
The Nushi's evolution is not random. If you use fire-based attacks frequently, the Nushi may evolve heat-resistant plating, forcing you to switch to physical or frost damage.
</Callout>
