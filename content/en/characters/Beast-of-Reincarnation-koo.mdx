<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Beast of Reincarnation Koo Command System">

In the devastated landscapes of 4026 Japan, mastering the bond between Emma and her companion is the key to survival, and this **Beast of Reincarnation koo** guide provides the tactical depth needed to conquer the blight. Unlike traditional action RPGs where companions act independently, Koo is an extension of your tactical arsenal. While Emma handles the high-speed katana strikes and defensive maneuvers, Koo provides the heavy-hitting abilities and utility required to break enemy defenses.

The combat loop is defined by the "Command Point" system. You cannot simply spam Koo’s abilities; they must be earned through skillful play. By performing a **Just-Guard**, Emma deflects incoming damage and instantly generates points. These points are then spent within the command menu, which slows the game to a crawl, giving you the breathing room to select the perfect counter-measure for the current threat.

| Action | Result | Resource Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Emma's Light Attack** | Fast chip damage | Generates minor points |
| **Just-Guard Parry** | Negates damage, staggers enemies | **Large Point Gain** |
| **Koo's Offensive Skill** | High burst or AoE damage | Consumes 1-3 Points |
| **Koo's Support Skill** | Buffs Emma or heals | Consumes 2 Points |
| **Falling Assassination** | High initial stealth damage | No cost, setup required |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: The Triangle Advantage">
On PlayStation 5, the Triangle button is your gateway to tactical superiority. Always keep an eye on your point gauge; if it's full, you are wasting potential damage. Use the slow-motion effect of the menu to identify which enemy is winding up for an unblockable attack.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Defeating Nushi">

The Nushi are the primary bosses of *Beast of Reincarnation*. These giant malefacts are responsible for the "Blighted Forests" that transform the world in real-time. Defeating them is the only way to progress the story and unlock the full potential of the **Beast of Reincarnation koo** partnership.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Heart of the Blight">
    Navigate through the shifting forest terrain. Use Emma’s plant-like hair to reach elevated positions and scout the Nushi’s lair from above.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Analyze Attack Patterns">
    Nushi usually have two phases. In the first phase, focus entirely on **Emma’s parries**. Do not commit to long combos until you have built at least 3 Command Points.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Koo's Techniques">
    Once the Nushi is mid-animation, open the command menu. Select a technique that targets the Nushi’s specific weakness (revealed through exploration or Spirit Stone effects).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Absorb the Power">
    After the Nushi falls, Emma will absorb its blight. This unlocks a new branch on the skill tree, granting Koo more devastating abilities for the next region.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Nushi Type | Primary Habitat | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Forest Nushi** | Overgrown Ruins | Use verticality to avoid ground slams |
| **Wasteland Nushi** | Open Plains | Focus on ranged Koo commands |
| **Urban Nushi** | Ruined Cities | Use Emma's hair for rapid repositioning |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Synergy">
The most effective way to down a Nushi is the "Parry-Command-Strike" loop. Parry the boss's heavy hit, use Koo's stun command, and follow up with Emma's high-damage katana finishers while the boss is incapacitated.
</Callout>

</Section>
