<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Narrative Characters and Combat Synergy">

The heart of the **Beast of Reincarnation story** lies in the relationship between Emma and Ku. This partnership translates directly into a hybrid combat system where swordplay and tactical commands merge. Emma utilizes fast katana strikes, parries, and a wrist-mounted crossbow. Meanwhile, Ku acts as an active combat partner who can freeze time to execute powerful elemental and physical skills.

| Character | Weapon/Role | Primary Combat Mechanics | Narrative Background |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Emma** | Katana & Crossbow | Fast combos, generous parry windows, hair-based traversal | A deadly swordmaster searching for answers in quarantine zones. |
| **Ku** | Tactical Companion | Slow-motion tactical skills, auto-attacks, item detection | A loyal dog bonded with Emma through dramatic past events. |

This combat system relies on a dual-resource loop. To activate Ku's powerful abilities, Emma must generate fluorescent points by executing successful parries.

<Section icon={Target} title="Campaign Progression and Exploration Guide">

Progression in the **Beast of Reincarnation story** is structured around large, open-ended sandbox areas called quarantine zones rather than a fully open world. Each zone contains unique environmental puzzles, hidden treasure chests, and a powerful regional boss.

### Traversal with Emma's Hair
Emma's hair is a versatile tool for both exploration and stealth. By manipulating her hair, she can bypass obstacles and gain high-ground advantages over enemies.

| Traversal Ability | How to Execute | Tactical Advantage |
|---|---|---|
| **Grappling Vine** | Tap grapple button | Whip to distant ledges quickly |
| **Hair Stilts** | Hold jump button | Reach high platforms easily |
| **Log Platforms** | Hold right trigger | Create bridges for stealth drops |

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Mobile Base and Flashback Sequences">

Between excursions into the quarantine zones, Emma and Ku return to their mobile base. This hub serves as a safe haven where players clean Ku's fur, cook meals, craft gear, and manage their loadouts. More importantly, the mobile base acts as the primary device for advancing the **Beast of Reincarnation story** through playable flashback sequences.

| Base Activity | Gameplay Benefit | Narrative Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Fur Grooming** | Cleans blood off Ku | Strengthens companion bond |
| **Playable Flashbacks** | Solo swordplay practice | Reveals how Emma and Ku met |
| **Equipment Workshop** | Upgrade weapons and armor | Prepares players for harder zones |

The flashback sequences temporarily remove Ku from your party. This forces you to master Emma's core swordplay and parry mechanics without relying on companion abilities to bail you out.

<Callout type="info" title="Story Insight">
Flashbacks are vital for understanding the origins of the blight and the history of the world before the quarantine zones were established.
</Callout>
