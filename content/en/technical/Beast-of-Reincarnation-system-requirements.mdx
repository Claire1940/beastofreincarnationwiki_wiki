<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Systems & Gameplay Mechanics">

The combat in Beast of Reincarnation deviates heavily from Game Freak's traditional turn-based formula, offering a robust real-time hack-and-slash system with deep tactical layers. Players control Emma, a Sealer wielding a katana and ranged weapons, alongside her wolf companion Co.

### Tactical Blooming Arts
By successfully performing parries, you fill Co's gauge. When activated, time slows down, allowing you to select powerful "Blooming Arts" from a radial menu. This hybrid system blends fast action with tactical decision-making, reminiscent of modern action RPG formulas.

| Combat Mechanic | Input / Trigger | Gameplay Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perfect Parry** | Timed L1/LB block | Fills Co's gauge, builds stagger on enemies |
| **Blooming Arts** | Slows time via radial menu | Deals massive damage, applies status effects |
| **Overdrive** | Press L3 + R3 (Max gauge) | Activates Entanglement Overdrive for burst DPS |
| **Companion Command** | Left Bumper + D-pad | Directs Co to hold position or attack targets |

### Elemental Strengths and Weaknesses
Paying attention to elemental affinities is crucial for late-game stages. Enemies display their active weaknesses next to their health bars.

| Element Type | Primary Effect | Best Used Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Poison** | Damage over time | Organic Malefax beasts |
| **Corrosion** | Reduces armor/defense | Golem robot constructs |
| **Burn** | High damage, prevents healing | Plant-based hybrids |
| **Shock** | Stuns and interrupts actions | Fast-moving agile targets |
